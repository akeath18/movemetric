The Movemetric Suite

Movemetric is a growing suite of apps designed for K–12 physical education teachers, schools, districts, and families. It helps turn everyday activity and fitness testing into data you can use to support learning and wellness.

ClassMetrics

Run standardized fitness tests, log results fast, and export clear reports.

  • Standard K–12 fitness tests in one place.
  • Built for large classes and tight schedules.
  • Exports reports for students, parents, and admins.

Platforms: iPad (teachers), CSV/PDF exports.

ClassMetrics HR

Stream live heart-rate data from student devices and monitor time in activity zones.

  • Live tiles for each student.
  • Tracks time in key heart-rate zones.
  • Stores sessions for documentation.

Designed for use with compatible heart-rate sensors.

FitAI

Smart wellness coaching for teachers, students, and families. Set goals, track progress, and receive AI-guided activity and wellness recommendations.

  • Personalized activity and wellness plans.
  • Goal tracking for movement and habits.
  • Supports school and family wellness initiatives.

Built to work both inside and outside the PE setting.

Additional apps and integrations are under development as Movemetric partners with schools and districts.

Apps built for PE and school wellness

ClassMetrics – Fitness assessment made manageable.

ClassMetrics helps physical education teachers manage the entire fitness testing process—from building classes to running tests to exporting reports. It’s built for real gyms, noisy environments, and limited prep time.

  • Library of standard fitness tests used in K–12 programs.
  • Fast score logging designed for large rosters.
  • Student profiles with multi-year history.
  • Automated class and individual reports.
  • Exports to spreadsheets and PDFs.

ClassMetrics HR – Real-time activity data for your classes.

ClassMetrics HR connects to compatible heart-rate devices so teachers can see how active their students are during class. It focuses on time in target zones, supporting MVPA documentation and instructional decisions.

  • Connects to multiple heart-rate devices for full-class monitoring.
  • Displays live heart-rate tiles for each student.
  • Shows time spent in light, moderate, and vigorous zones.
  • Saves sessions for reflection and reporting.
  • Exports summary data for classes and individuals.
Together with ClassMetrics, ClassMetrics HR gives you both outcome data (fitness scores) and process data (activity during lessons), all aligned with your curriculum.

FitAI – Smart wellness coaching for your community.

FitAI is a personal wellness app that supports teachers, students, and families with AI-guided activity and habit coaching. It can stand alone as a community wellness tool or complement the data you see in ClassMetrics and ClassMetrics HR.

  • Helps users set movement, wellness, or weight-management goals.
  • Builds adaptive plans based on user input and progress.
  • Encourages daily activity, recovery, and healthy routines.
  • Suitable for teacher wellness programs and family use.
FitAI extends Movemetric beyond the gym, giving your school community a smart companion for everyday wellness.
