Apps built for PE teachers

ClassMetrics – Fitness assessment made manageable.

ClassMetrics helps physical education teachers manage the entire fitness testing process—from building classes to running tests to exporting reports. It’s built for real gyms, noisy environments, and limited prep time.

  • Library of standard fitness tests used in K–12 programs.
  • Fast score logging designed for large rosters.
  • Student profiles with multi-year history.
  • Automated class and individual reports.
  • Exports to spreadsheets and PDFs.

ClassMetrics HR – Real-time activity data for your classes.

ClassMetrics HR connects to compatible heart‑rate devices so teachers can see how active their students are during class. It focuses on time in target zones, supporting MVPA documentation and instructional decisions.

  • Connects to multiple heart‑rate devices for full‑class monitoring.
  • Displays live heart‑rate tiles for each student.
  • Shows time spent in light, moderate, and vigorous zones.
  • Saves sessions for reflection and reporting.
  • Exports summary data for classes and individuals.
Together with ClassMetrics, ClassMetrics HR gives you both outcome data (fitness scores) and process data (activity during lessons), all aligned with your curriculum.
